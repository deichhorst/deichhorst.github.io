About

Dexter Eichhorst was born in Koror, Palau in 1995 but has lived in the San Francisco Bay Area since 1996. From an early age, Dexter has had a passion for sports: particularly soccer and lacrosse. In 2014, Dexter graduated Marin Academy high school in San Rafael, California and was subsequently accepted to Tufts University where he played on the 2014 National Championship winning Men's Varsity Soccer team. Dexter is currently pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Cognitive and Brain Scienes with an emphasis in Computer Science.
